API
===

Get an API key with the purchase of one of our devices or go to `speedbocktech.com <https://www.speedbocktech.com/api>`_ **API** section to request for one. 
Our team will respond within 1 to 2 bussiness days.

With Speedbock's API, you can get quick and reliable access to our online sensor database.  

Base url: `<https://api.speedbocktech.com/api/v1>`_

Limits
------

   - up to 50 results per returned calls
   - up to 30 calls per minute
   - up to 1,000 calls per day

.. warning::

   If you need more calls contact Speedbock to share your needs for your project. Send an email to `contact <mailto:contact@speedbocktech.com>`_.
